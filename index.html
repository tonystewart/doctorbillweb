DoctorBill© for practices


DoctorBill© is your simple and secure gateway for accepting Visa, Mastercard and BPay® payments for your professional services

  • - No need for your own merchant account - you will like our simple application process!
  • - No setup or ongoing fees, just the service fee for processed payments - no charge if you don't use the service
  • - No contract lock-ins or penalties if you decide to end the service
  • - No hardware terminals, and no software to install!
  • - Patients make payments at your customised DoctorBill web page, or via a 1300 number. Practice staff can take payment via a Virtual Terminal
  • - You are informed by email and optionally by SMS when a payment is made
  • - Funds settle directly to your nominated account once they clear. Payment is for the gross amount, fees are periodically deducted separately
  • - DoctorBill© is powered by Ezidebit, so you are protected by the highest level of internet payments security. Ezidebit operates under an Australian Financial Services licence (no. 315388) with Westpac Banking Corporation sponsorship

DoctorBill© Web Pay


Web Pay allows you to accept Mastercard and Visa payments, with Amex and Diners available at your request.
Payments can be taken in 3 ways:

  1. - Practice staff can use a Virtual Terminal window on any internet connected computer to take card payments.
  2. - You can print Web Pay instructions on your invoices, to direct your patients to your customised payment page where they can pay your invoice.
  3. - If you have a website you can include a link to the above payments page for your patients to use.
  • You will be automaticlly notified of any Web Pay payments, and funds will settle to your nominated account once processed.

  • If your Practice Management Software has DoctorBill integration, Web Pay payments will reconcile direclty to the appropriate invoice.

DoctorBill© Phone Pay


Web Pay allows you to accept Mastercard and Visa payments, with Amex and Diners available at your request.
Payments can be taken in 3 ways:

  1. - Usually, your patients would access the Phone Pay service by dialing 1300 369 187..
  2. - If you have a website, you can provide the above phone number on your payments page.
  3. - If your practice phone/PABX allows automated diversions (e.g. "Press 4 to make a payment") you can divert the call to 07 3124 5580 to access Phone Pay.
  • Patients will hear an Interactive Voice Recording (IVR) that steps them through the payment process.
  • DoctorBill automatically checks daily for any payments via this channel and sends you an email notifying you of the phone payment(s) for the previous day. Funds will settle to your nominated account once processed.

  • If your Practice Management Software has DoctorBill integration, Phone Pay payments will reconcile direclty to the appropriate invoice.
